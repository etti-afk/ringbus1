Bus Types and Designs

Understanding Bus types is the first step to custom designs according to objectives. In optimizations, there is always a bargain. In this particular item, there are many designs and even more tradeoffs. The simplest of bus designs sacrifices speed for streamlined accuracy.

Method of Arbitration

Optimization methods varies between projects and objectives accordingly. In this we want to position the advantages in a way such that these advantages remain in the projects favor according to what it is meant to do. In this, many things can go wrong. For one, electrical interference can slow or modify the rate of transfer, the accuracy of data signal, and the warp of transmision. Interference between wires can be minimized by increasing the distance between wires. Given the current rate of technology and the ability to shrink wire diameters, interference is not much an issue with secure bus designs.

Timing

Timing in bus types are either analytical or digital. Both have their advantages and disadvantages; currently, the most widely used is digital because of its simplicity and standardization in technology.

Bus Width

Bus Width is the factor which the bus can carry data packets. The wider the bus, the more data it is able to transfer in time. The tradeoff in this is speed for amount.
